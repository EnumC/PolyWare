About

PolyWare is an annual hackathon that aims to foster new and impactful hardware developments that will benefit the world of tomorrow. In addition to helping students improve their design and development skills, we also provide support to help students improve business and presentation skills- not just to pitch a successful project, but also to inspire the next generation of inventors, entrepreneurs, and leaders. Food, workshops, prizes, and swag will be provided to all participants for free.
